Our Mission

“We are a group of young ambitious individuals, motivated to change the world.”

Welcome! We are Jessie and Joanna, two young women in university with great aspirations for the future. However, back in high school, despite our aspirational dreams, we did not know how to harness our potential or build our skillsets to become future leaders. Since problems often lead to the birth of opportunities, this made us realize there must be many other young adults who also feel the same type of ambition and aspirations but feel confused or lost about how to nurture their ability and mentality.

This inspired us to embark on our Boss-Up journey where we can bring together leaders and inspirations to help nurture leadership potential in fellow young adults.

To achieve this goal, we have organized two conferences, Miss Boss Conference and Boss-Up Summit, where we brought together inspiring leaders in different fields to share their insight and guidance on leadership with hundreds of youths.

In the future, we hope to expand and create a multimedia platform where we can help and inspire more young adults in harnessing the leaders within them.

We are honored and humbled to have you join us on this path. Together we can create a better future tomorrow!
